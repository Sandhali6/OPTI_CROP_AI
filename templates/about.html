🌱 About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an Artificial Intelligence powered Crop Recommendation System developed to assist farmers in selecting the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions.



🎯 Our Mission

To help farmers improve productivity by recommending the best crop using Machine Learning and environmental data.

🤖 AI Technology

The system analyzes Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Temperature, Humidity, pH and Rainfall to generate accurate crop recommendations.

🌾 Smart Agriculture

AI-driven recommendations help reduce farming risks, improve crop yield, and support sustainable agriculture.



📌 How OptiCrop Works

1️⃣ Farmer enters soil nutrient values (N, P, K).

2️⃣ Environmental parameters such as Temperature, Humidity, Rainfall and pH are provided.

3️⃣ Data is standardized using the trained scaler.

4️⃣ The Machine Learning model predicts the most suitable crop.

5️⃣ The recommended crop is displayed instantly.
